Sponsors

No sponsors for the 2018/2019 Year. Be the first now!

Our club is small and relatively new, but we're dedicated and are united by our love of all things rockets. We're raising funds to help our mission of making our own rocket to participate in the NASA SLI competition in Huntsville, Alabama! In the first couple weeks of April our team will hopefully be participating, along with dozens of other university teams from across the country, in a rocket competition at the NASA Marshall Space Flight Center.

We're asking for donations to help us cover costs for the competition including parts for our rocket, tools, and team travel costs for launches and the competition. Frankly, we’re pretty short on funds, and we’re primarily funded through donations and our own pockets, so we could use all the help we can get!

We can place a sticker of you company's logo (or your face or whatever) on the rocket and your logo on this website to promote your company and show your support. We can also make a LinkedIn or Instagram post thanking you and/or your company. We are very grateful for any and all contributions! Please message us if you are interested.
