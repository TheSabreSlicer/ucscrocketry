Our Goal

This year, our team will be competing in the 2018 NASA Student Launch Competition. This will require the development of a high powered model rocket capable of delivering an experimental payload to an altitude of exactly 1 mile.

The Rocket Team at UC Santa Cruz was selected to participate in the NASA Student Launch Initiative this year, for the first time. The NASA Student Initiative is a national collegiate competition with specific technical challenges that culminates in an advanced high powered model rocket with onboard electronics. Our mission is to promote and perform research in the field of aeronautics, with a focus on the development of a rocket capable of supporting small scientific payloads.The team is currently partnering with the Earth Science Department to perform novel research on cloud turbulence. The team provides a real-world STEM learning experience for the students involved, and the larger community through outreach to local schools. We are fostering an environment that immerses and trains students in the professional world of STEM.
