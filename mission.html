Mission

To provide an empowering, interdisciplinary, and hands-on educational experience to its members and the community.

We create an inclusive learning environment united by our shared passion for aerospace and engineering.

In just one quarter, we recruited a team of passionate students who built, designed, and launched an 8-foot-tall high-power rocket. This year, we will be launching Effective-1 at the NASA Student Launch Initiative in April. We also plan to perform unique atmospheric research with a professor in the Earth Science department on cloud turbulence.
